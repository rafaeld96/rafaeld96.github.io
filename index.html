About Me

Hi again! I'm Rafael, a software enthusiast and avid gamer, and I've been making games since I was 8. It began with using the Level Editor to whatever game I could get my hands on, one of the first being Hannah and the Pirate Caves. Gradually, I was exposed to code as in Neverwinter Nights where I wanted to replicate features of the campaigns, which employed C++ code. With time, the complexity of the projects grew and among other things I managed to provide a much-appreciated gameplay experience with Confusion: An atmospheric custom story.

Since high-school I've been studying at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. Whenever I become complacent I take on extra work and have thus worked part-time extensively during my studies. I am extremely interested in ideas and love to explore new topics and fields.

Please feel free to _contact_ me if you have any questions.
